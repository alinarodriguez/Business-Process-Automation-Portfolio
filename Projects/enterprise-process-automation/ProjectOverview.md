# Enterprise Process Automation Portfolio

## Project Context

Over several years, multiple internal operational processes across different departments were digitized and automated using Microsoft Power Platform and SharePoint.

These solutions replaced manual processes that relied heavily on email communication, spreadsheets, and informal approvals.

The initiative resulted in the development of more than 100 internal automation solutions used by over 500 employees across multiple business areas.

---

## Business Problem

Many operational processes within organizations rely on manual coordination, including:

- Service requests
- Approval workflows
- Internal permissions
- Employee administrative processes
- Operational tracking

These processes often lack:

- Visibility
- Traceability
- Standardization
- Centralized data management

As organizations grow, these inefficiencies significantly impact operational productivity.

---

## Solution Approach

A structured automation strategy was implemented using Microsoft Power Platform to digitize and standardize internal processes.

Each solution followed a consistent methodology:

1. Business process analysis  
2. Definition of business rules and validation logic  
3. User interface design  
4. Workflow automation  
5. Data persistence and traceability  
6. User documentation and adoption  

This approach ensured consistency across multiple internal tools.
